Problem and requirments

Altius is a web and marketing agency in Macedonia, that works with both macedonian and foreign clients. In a time of pandemic they are aware that more and more people want to sell online. So they approached us with a task to create e-commerce template websites that could be easily modified and can serve any industry.

UX research

Through analyzing the user personas, we we re trying to figure how to make a website that could be easily used by any age range and industry. Another main key point was making components that could be transformed or re-combined in order to get multiple variatons.

Product design

We came up with two different templates, and even though they used the same elements, they have a completely different look and feel. Along with this we came up with multiple variations how the components could be combined.
